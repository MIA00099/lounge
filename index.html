THE CHILLAX EXPERIENCE

Casual. Upscale.

Located in the vibrant Nyarutarama district, Chillax Lounge is celebrated for its stunning modern architecture and breathtaking views of Kigali.

We seamlessly transition from a relaxed, romantic dining atmosphere in the early evening to a high-energy nightclub as the night unfolds. Whether you're here for our signature cocktails, diverse menu, or the pulsing Afrobeats, the vibe is always impeccable.
